About Sahara Energy

Sahara Energy Limited was incorporated in 2014 with the aim of being the energy provider of choice in the region, and ultimately, Africa. Our registered office and headquarters are in Nairobi, Kenya.

Our core business is the importation, distribution and marketing of refined fuel products to all our markets in the region, performed by a team comprised of highly experienced professionals in the oil and gas space.
