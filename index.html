About Sahara Energy

Sahara Energy Limited was incorporated in 2014 with the aim of being the energy provider of choice in the region, and ultimately, Africa. Our registered office and headquarters are in Nairobi, Kenya.

Our core business is the importation, distribution and marketing of refined fuel products to all our markets in the region, performed by a team comprised of highly experienced professionals in the oil and gas space.

Business Model

Commercial and Industrial

We play a significant part in the B2B segment, with local and export sales of our fuel products. We provide fuel to companies operating in key sectors the economy, i.e. road construction and manufacturing, thus playing a critical role in the growth of the economic system in the region.


Supply and Trading

We leverage on our strong relationships with global traders to get the best possible pricing for our customers. Our expertise spanning several years in downstream and upstream sectors gives us an edge in providing fuel to the region reliably.
